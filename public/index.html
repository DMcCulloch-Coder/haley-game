GO, GO, GO

Set up:

Each player must have an Index card with a color on it. Every player needs $200 to begin the game each player rolls a six-sided dice and the player who rolls the highest number goes first and so on…


Players:

Game is for 2-3 players. Ages 10+


Getting the Game Started:

The first player will have someone else pick up a blue card for them and the player who picks it up will read the question to them, they will then have to answer the question to their best ability if they get it right the card says what they can do, if they get it wrong then they will have to roll the six-sided dice and move back however many times it says to.


Meanings:

Steal - take someone else’s Amusement park but you have to pay at least $50 or more.

Moving Back - Going backward rather than being able to move forward.

Moving forward - Keep on going the way you were going.


Playing the Whole Game:

First playing chooses someone to grab the top card for them and that player reads the card out loud and the first player must try their hardest to answer the question if they get it right they do what the card says to do if the player gets it wrong then they have to roll a six-sided dice and move back however many times it says to and do that action if they land on a place that they want to buy they can buy it for how much it says on the placemat, However, if someone owns that place you have to pay a $50 fee and the next player does the same thing. The game ends when every place is sold and the person with the most places wins.
